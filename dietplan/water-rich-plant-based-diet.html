Water-Rich, Plant-Based Diet

Understanding the 70/30 Ratio

The human body is 70% water and 30% solids. This balance supports vital functions such as temperature regulation, nutrient transport, and energy metabolism. Maintaining hydration and balanced nutrition is essential for overall health.

The Importance of Water-Rich Foods

Water-rich foods support hydration, digestion, and detoxification. Prioritize these foods in your diet for balanced energy and wellness.

Raw & Sun-Cooked Foods: Energy from Nature

Raw foods retain enzymes and nutrients, which are often destroyed by cooking. Aim to consume at least 70% raw foods, using gentle cooking methods when needed.
