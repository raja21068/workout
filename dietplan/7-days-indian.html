7-Day Meal Plan

Day 1 Day 2 Day 3 Day 4 Day 5 Day 6 Day 7
Breakfast Poha with vegetables and a sprinkle of peanuts. Greek yogurt with sliced banana, walnuts, and jaggery. Smoothie with spinach, mango, almond milk, and chia seeds. Whole-grain toast with avocado and scrambled tofu. Chia seed pudding with almond milk, berries, and honey. Besan chilla with onions, tomatoes, and green chilies. Masala oats with vegetables and coriander leaves.
Snack Apple with a handful of roasted chana. Mixed nuts (almonds, cashews). Boiled chickpeas with black salt and lemon juice. Seasonal fruit with chaat masala. Roasted almonds and raisins. Mixed fruit (pomegranate, apple, banana). Fruit salad with walnuts.
Lunch Rajma with brown rice and a mixed vegetable salad. Moong dal with quinoa or brown rice, and sautéed green beans. Chana masala with whole-wheat roti and a mixed green salad. Bhindi masala, dal with whole-wheat roti, and salad. Kala chana curry with brown rice and spinach salad. Palak dal with quinoa and cucumber-tomato salad. Quinoa pulao with black beans, mixed vegetables, and yogurt.
Snack Carrot and cucumber sticks with mint chutney. Roasted makhana with chaat masala. Celery sticks with green chutney. Greek yogurt with roasted flaxseeds. Cottage cheese with black pepper. Protein smoothie with spinach, banana, and almond milk. Bell pepper slices with hummus.
Dinner Paneer tikka with sautéed spinach and whole-wheat roti. Vegetable pulao with tofu, raita, and cucumber slices. Daal tadka with brown rice and cucumber raita. Mixed vegetable curry with basmati rice and cucumber slices. Aloo gobi with whole-wheat roti and fresh salad. Vegetable khichdi with ghee and roasted papad. Baingan bharta with whole-wheat roti and a green salad.
